## Trigger Condition
A student is placed on probation if their CGPA falls below **2.0**.
Applies to all students enrolled from **Summer 2022 onward**.

## Semester Drop During Probation
Not permitted, except in cases of serious illness or major circumstances
beyond the student's control. Requires approval from the Head of Department
and the Registrar, with medical evidence confirmed by the university Medical Centre.

## Dismissal Condition
Failure to achieve CGPA 2.0 within the allowed probationary semesters
results in consideration for **withdrawal from the university**.

## Appeal / Exception
The Dean or Head of Department may recommend to the Pro-Vice-Chancellor
that the student continue for a further defined period. Grounds include:
- Serious illness or major adverse circumstances
- Borderline failure (CGPA just under 2.0)
- Positive overall student profile

## Old Policy (pre-Summer 2022)
Minimum CGPA was 1.5. Dismissal after failing to raise CGPA in 2 consecutive semesters.
Students with GPA below 1.0 in first semester could be asked to withdraw.
