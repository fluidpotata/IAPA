## Retake (Failed Course)
A **retake** applies when a student receives an **F grade**.

- Allowed up to **2 retakes** per course.
- Exception: Head of Department or Dean may authorize a **3rd retake** for
  students on probation to safeguard progression.
- Non-credit courses (091/092): only **1 retake** allowed.
- Further attempts beyond the limit require Head of Department + Registrar
  approval, only on grounds of major adverse circumstances (e.g. serious illness).
- **Grade counted:** best of all attempts.

## Repeat (Grade Improvement)
A **repeat** applies when a student wants to improve a passing grade.

- Allowed **once** per course.
- Must repeat within **2 semesters** of the original enrollment.
- No cap on the grade that can be earned.
- **Grade counted:** latest grade replaces original in CGPA calculation.

## Key Distinction
| | Retake | Repeat |
|---|---|---|
| Reason | F grade | Improve passing grade |
| Max attempts | 2 (3 by exception) | 1 |
| Time limit | None specified | Within 2 semesters |
| Grade used | Best | Latest |
